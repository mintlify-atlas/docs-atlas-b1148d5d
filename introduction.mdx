KolkataRail Intelligence is an AI-powered safety and crowd intelligence platform built for Kolkata suburban railway commuters and railway safety officers. It fuses real-time crowd reports, historical data, trend signals, and weather conditions to surface crowd risk levels per train — and provides a conversational, multilingual assistant for filing FIR complaints and managing them through a police dashboard.

## Who it's for

- **Commuters** — check crowd levels before boarding, search trains by route or intermediate station, and file incident complaints in English, Hindi, or Bengali.
- **Railway safety officers and police** — triage and manage submitted complaints, view analytics, and track FIR status through the police dashboard.

## Key capabilities

| Capability | Service | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Crowd prediction | Crowd Intelligence | Fuses historical, trend, live, and weather signals into GREEN / YELLOW / RED crowd levels |
| Train search | Crowd Intelligence | Finds trains between stations with direct, reverse, and via-stop support using Neo4j |
| FIR filing | NirbhayaSathi | Conversational complaint filing with auto-extracted incident details |
| Police dashboard | NirbhayaSathi | Manage, triage, and analyze submitted complaints |
| AI categorization | NirbhayaSathi | Groq LLM + RAG for complaint category suggestions and detail extraction |

## Language support

The platform supports **English**, **Hindi**, and **Bengali** through the `langdetect` and `deep-translator` libraries. NirbhayaSathi detects the user's language automatically and responds in kind throughout the FIR session.

<Note>
  KolkataRail Intelligence includes a `manifest.json` for Progressive Web App (PWA) installation. Users on mobile can install the app to their home screen via the browser's "Add to home screen" prompt, with shortcuts to file a complaint or check crowd levels directly.
</Note>
